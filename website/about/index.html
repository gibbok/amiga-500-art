Portfolio Notes

These are some of the artworks I created in 1991 on my Amiga 500 Plus. I mainly used Deluxe Paint and Brilliance, with additional post-processing in Amiga BASIC.

At the time, my Amiga had two floppy drives and 2 MB of RAM.

The images in this portfolio were recovered from VHS backups recorded from my Amiga. This is why some of them appear slightly blurred.
